Corporate Information

Eng. Prabath Wickramasinghe is Electrical Engineer by profession (Univ. of Peradeniya 1995-1999). He also holds Management Qualifications from Chartered Institute of Management Accountancy (CIMA) of UK (1993-1998) & qualified in Marketing from Chartered Institute of Marketing UK (1993-1998). Eng. Wickramasinghe has extensive experience in Development & Marketing renewable energy especially small hydro and wind power since year 2000. He has worked in government utility owned subsidiary and also in a leading multinational conglomerate before starting his own company Pacific Hydro Electric SL (Pvt) Limited between 2005-2010. Eng. Wickramasinghe has involved in development, planning, construction and commissioning and operating more than 25 utility scale renewable power plants which includes large number of small hydro, wind, solar and bio-mass power plants during the last 20 years.

Eng. Asanka Tennakoon is an Electrical Engineer by profession (Univ. of Peradeniya 1995-1999). He holds a Master Degree from UK on Information Systems and Post Graduate Diploma in Geographical Information Systems & Remote Sensing (GIS& RS). Eng. Asanka is one of the most experienced Solar Energy Engineers in Sri Lanka with 7 years of hands on Experience. He was the person who installed the First Ever Net Metering System in Sri Lanka way back in July 2010. Eng. Asanka was the project manager for Eluvaithivu mini grid Project which is Sri Lankaâ€™s first solar, wind & Diesel powered mini grid off the coast of Kytes, Jaffna which is one of the most challenging assignment he had carried out so far. Eluvaithivu island is located 8km from the Jaffna mainland towards South-West in Indian Ocean.
